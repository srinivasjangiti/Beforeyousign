FREELANCE SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Freelance Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2025, between ABC Company, Inc. ("Client") and Jane Doe ("Contractor").

1. SERVICES
Contractor agrees to provide web development services as outlined in project specifications. Specific deliverables and timelines will be documented in separate project proposals approved by both parties.

2. COMPENSATION
2.1 Contractor shall be paid at the rate of $100 per hour for services performed.
2.2 Invoices will be submitted bi-weekly and payment is due within 30 days of receipt.
2.3 Late payments will incur 1.5% monthly interest after 30 days.
2.4 Client will reimburse pre-approved expenses with proper documentation.

3. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS
Contractor is an independent contractor, not an employee. Contractor is responsible for own taxes, insurance, and benefits. Contractor may serve other clients.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
4.1 Work Product: Upon full payment, Client owns all deliverables created specifically for Client under this Agreement.
4.2 Pre-existing Materials: Contractor retains ownership of all pre-existing materials, templates, and tools. Client receives a non-exclusive license to use such materials as incorporated in deliverables.
4.3 Portfolio Use: Contractor may display work in portfolio and promotional materials unless Client reasonably designates project as confidential.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary information disclosed during the engagement. This obligation survives for 3 years after termination. Confidential information does not include information that is publicly available or independently developed.

6. TERM AND TERMINATION
6.1 This Agreement begins on the date above and continues until terminated.
6.2 Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice.
6.3 Client may terminate immediately for material breach, with Contractor having 10 days to cure.
6.4 Upon termination, Client pays for all work completed through termination date.
6.5 Contractor will deliver all work in progress and return Client materials.

7. WARRANTIES AND REPRESENTATIONS
7.1 Contractor warrants that work will be performed in a professional manner consistent with industry standards.
7.2 Contractor warrants that deliverables will not infringe third-party intellectual property rights.
7.3 Contractor will correct defects in deliverables for 90 days after delivery at no charge.

8. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
8.1 Contractor's total liability shall not exceed the amount paid by Client in the 6 months preceding the claim.
8.2 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages.
8.3 This limitation does not apply to breaches of confidentiality or intellectual property violations.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
9.1 The parties will attempt to resolve disputes through good faith negotiation.
9.2 If negotiation fails, parties agree to mediation before pursuing litigation.
9.3 This Agreement is governed by [Reasonable State] law.
9.4 Venue for any legal action is courts in [Neutral Location].
9.5 Prevailing party in litigation may recover reasonable attorney fees.

10. GENERAL PROVISIONS
10.1 Changes to this Agreement must be in writing and signed by both parties.
10.2 This Agreement may be assigned only with written consent of the other party.
10.3 If any provision is invalid, the remaining provisions remain in effect.
10.4 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

Both parties have read and agree to the terms of this Agreement.
